RUBBER TREE

This is a plant that can grow quite big, living up to its name rubber tree. So if you’ve got the room these beauties could be a wonderful addition to your space. Just make sure you plant them in a pot big enough for it to grow into a 4 foot tall tree!

If you want to add a little more spice to your space you could opt for the variegated variety of rubber tree. These plants have many shades of green and dapples of white on their leaves. Sometimes they have a bit of pink as well!

CARE SHEET

SUN: bright-indirect light
WATER: moist in summer, dry in winter
SOIL: well-draining, well-aerated
TEMP: 60-85 °F
