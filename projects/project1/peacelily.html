PEACE LILY

If you struggle with knowing when is the right time to water your plants, this may be the plant for you. These plants are drama queens and they will let you know when they are thirsty by drooping over in the most theatrical way. If your peace lily is looking a little droopy, give it a good, deep water and it will perk back up in a couple of hours.

A special thing about peace lilies is that they reward really great care by blooming. These “flowers” are not actually flowers but a different form of their usual leaves. The most important factor in getting it to flower is having the perfect amount of sunlight.

CARE SHEET

SUN: bright-indirect light
WATER: about once weekly
SOIL: rich, loamy, well-draining
TEMP: 65-85 °F
